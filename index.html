About Me

Background

I am a 39 year old father of one from Birmingham, England.

I have worked in retail for the last 20 years but have always had an interest in computing. The birth of my daughter in 2010 spured me on to look into developing my interest in computing to give me more options going forward. To achieve this over the last few years I have been working towards a computing degree with the Open University (OU), which I will complete by 2019.

During my studies with the OU I have picked up a Diploma of Higher Education in Computing and IT (equivalent to the first two years of a degree).

Up until the start of 2017 I was undecided on what area of computing I wanted to focus on, but after completing some modules with the OU I found I enjoyed anything involving web technologies more than anything else, so my love of web development was born.

Currently

I am currently working through a Web Development Bootcamp by Colt Steele whilst I await the start of my final module with the OU later this year.

My final OU module is based around human and computer interaction which should help me develop good user-focus design on any future projects.

I am really enjoying the Web Development Bootcamp by Colt Steele and feel I have learnt a large amount in a short amount of time (It helped me make this).

Next Steps

  • Create projects (this site being one of them).
  • Apply for Junior Developer jobs.
